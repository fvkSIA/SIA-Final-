Full Job Description

Responsibilities:

  • Safely transport the employer and/or their family to various destinations, including but not limited to work, appointments, and social events.
  • Maintain a clean, well-maintained vehicle, conducting regular inspections and maintenance to ensure safety and reliability.
  • Plan efficient routes to minimize travel time while prioritizing safety and comfort.
  • Assist with loading and unloading luggage, groceries, and other items as needed.
  • Provide exceptional customer service, including opening doors, assisting with packages, and accommodating any special requests.
  • Maintain confidentiality and discretion regarding the employer's personal and professional matters.
  • Adhere to all traffic laws and regulations, and exercise caution in all driving situations.
  • Communicate effectively with the employer to coordinate schedules and make necessary adjustments.

Qualifications:

  • Valid driver's license with a clean driving record.
  • Proven experience as a personal or chauffeur driver, preferably in a similar capacity.
  • Excellent knowledge of local roads and routes, with the ability to navigate efficiently.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills, with a professional demeanor.
  • Ability to prioritize safety and maintain composure in stressful situations.
  • Flexibility to accommodate varying schedules and travel needs, including occasional evenings and weekends.
  • Punctuality and reliability are essential, with a commitment to meeting deadlines and appointments.
  • Discretion and respect for privacy are paramount, with the ability to maintain confidentiality.
