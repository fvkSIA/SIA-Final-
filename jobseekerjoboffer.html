Job Offer

Delivery Helper

Quezon City

Job details

Job type

Full-time

Salary

PHP 15,000 - PHP 18,000

Full Job description

Responsibilities:

  • Assist the primary driver in the delivery and unloading of products to various locations.
  • Ensure safe and timely transportation of goods, following the assigned route and schedule.
  • Load and unload products from the delivery vehicle.
  • Verify delivery items against invoices or shipping documents.
  • Provide excellent customer service by addressing client inquiries and concerns professionally.
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of the delivery vehicle.
  • Follow all safety protocols and company procedures during deliveries.
  • Assist in the regular maintenance and inspection of the delivery vehicle.

Qualifications:

  • Clean driving record with a valid driver's license.
  • Able to lift and carry heavy items (up to 50 lbs) regularly.
  • Familiarity with using road maps or GPS devices.
  • Strong attention to detail.
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Positive attitude and a proactive approach to work.

Skills:

  • Prior delivery or customer service experience is a plus.
  • Basic math skills.

Schedule:

  • Full-time.
  • Day shift.
  • Willing to work weekends.
  • Overtime as needed.

Supplemental Pay types:

  • Tips.

To Apply:

Interested applicants should send a resume and a brief cover letter to [email address] with the subject line 'Delivery Helper Driver Application.'
